# Research Publications

## 2025
- **FlareGS: 4D Flare Removal using Gaussian Splatting for Urban Scenes**  
  *Mayank Chandak, **K. Sai Sri Teja**, Rahul Gopi Raju Matta, Vinayak Gupta, Kaushik Mitra*  
  _ICCV Workshop 2025_  
  Proposed a multi-view flare removal framework using RGB-LiDAR fusion and Gaussian Splatting for robust perception in autonomous driving.  
  [Paper](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2025W/2COOOL/papers/Chandak_FlareGS_4D_Flare_Removal_using_Gaussian_Splatting_for_Urban_Scenes_ICCVW_2025_paper.pdf) | [Webpage](https://saisriteja.github.io/Flare4DGS/)

- **PhotonSplat: 3D Scene Reconstruction and Colorization from SPAD Sensors**  
  ***K. Sai Sri Teja***, Sreevidya Chintalapati*, Vinayak Gupta*, Mukund Varma T, Haejoon Lee, Aswin Sankaranarayanan, Kaushik Mitra*  
  _ICCP 2025_  
  Presented a novel approach to reconstruct and colorize 3D scenes from noisy SPAD sensor data, along with the PhotonScenes dataset.  
  [Paper](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2506.21680) | [Project Page](https://vinayak-vg.github.io/PhotonSplat/) | [Code](https://github.com/Vinayak-VG/PhotonSplat)
